About Us

Who are we

We are The Boring Club, a student organization at the University of Washington focused on helping underclassmen build foundational engineering skills through hands-on, project-based learning. From workshops on Git, soldering, and digital design to mentorship-driven skill-building modules, we create an inclusive space where students can gain practical experience, grow their resumes, and prepare for success in competitive engineering student organizations and future careers. If you have specific questions about our club, please email: theboringclub@uw.edu
